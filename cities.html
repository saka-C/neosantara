Indonesia City

Kota Tua

West Jakarta

Kota Tua Jakarta is a historic area located in West Jakarta. It is known for its old Dutch colonial buildings, museums, and the famous Fatahillah Square. This place shows the history and cultural heritage of Jakarta.

JL. Malioboro

Jl. Malioboro, Yogyakarta

Malioboro Street is a famous tourist area located in Yogyakarta. It is known for its shopping street, traditional food, street artists, and vibrant atmosphere. Malioboro represents the cultural life and traditions of Yogyakarta.

JL. Braga Bandung

Bandung, West Java

Braga Street is a historic street located in Bandung. Today, it is a popular tourist destination with cafés, restaurants, art galleries, and well-preserved colonial buildings. Braga Street combines historical charm with a modern urban lifestyle.

Lawang Sewu Semarang

Lawang Sewu, Semarang

Lawang Sewu is a historic building located in Semarang, Central Java. It is famous for its many doors and its Dutch colonial architecture. Today, Lawang Sewu is a popular tourist attraction and a museum that showcases Indonesian railway history.
