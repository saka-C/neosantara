Jl. Braga Bandung

Braga Street in Bandung is a historic area known for its colonial buildings, cafés, and artistic atmosphere.

History of Jl.Braga Bandung

Braga Street is one of the oldest streets in Bandung. During the Dutch colonial period, it was known as a prestigious shopping and entertainment area for Europeans, often called the “Paris of Java.” The street was filled with luxury shops, cafés, and cinemas. Today, Braga Street remains a historical area that preserves its colonial architecture while serving as a popular tourist destination.

Architecture

The uniqueness of Braga Street lies in its well-preserved colonial architecture combined with a modern artistic lifestyle. The street is known for its art galleries, cafés, street performances, and creative atmosphere that reflects Bandung’s identity as a creative city.
